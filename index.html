About Contests

Our contests are held every 3 months and all students from CCI are invited to Participate. The competition comprises of 3 rounds. If a student is disqualified in the 1st round he/she have a chance to Participate in the 3rd round

If a student is disqualified in the 2nd round he/she will not be able to Participate in the 3rd round. However those successfully pass the 3rd round will have the opportunity to Participate in all contests available throughout the following year
